Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Race Tracker is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device, and we never collect, share, or sell your personal information.

What We Store

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your profile information (name, age, preferred distances)
  • Your race data (past and upcoming races including dates, times, distances, and notes)
  • App preferences and settings

How We Store It

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). Your data is never transmitted to external servers, and we have no access to your information. The data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

No Third-Party Tracking

Race Tracker does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We don't collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry. We don't use cookies, pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can view, edit, or delete your profile information at any time within the app
  • You can add, edit, or delete any race entries at any time
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all your data from your device
  • There are no backups of your data on our servers because we don't have any servers

iCloud Sync

Currently, Race Tracker does not sync data via iCloud or any other cloud service. Your data remains exclusively on your device. If you delete the app or switch devices, your data will not be transferred.

Children's Privacy

Race Tracker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification. All data stays on the device and is never transmitted to us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or update the effective date above. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Through the App Store support page
  • By visiting our website at https://anansadiya94.github.io/racetracker-landingpage

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any information, you automatically have complete control over your data. You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time directly within the app.
