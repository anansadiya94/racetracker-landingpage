Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Race Tracker is designed with your privacy in mind. All your data stays on your device, and we never collect, share, or sell your personal information.

What We Store

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your profile information (name, age, preferred distances)
  • Your race data (past and upcoming races)
  • App preferences and settings

How We Store It

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). Your data is never transmitted to external servers, and we have no access to your information.

No Third-Party Tracking

Race Tracker does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We don't collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data. You can edit or delete your information at any time within the app. Deleting the app will permanently remove all your data from your device.

iCloud Sync

Currently, Race Tracker does not sync data via iCloud or any other cloud service. Your data remains exclusively on your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify you through the app or update the date below. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through the App Store or via our support channels.
